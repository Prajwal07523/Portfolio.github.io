Route Optimization for Delivery using Graph Algorithms

Route optimization is essential for quick e-commerce platforms to ensure timely and cost-effective deliveries. Graph algorithms, particularly the A* (A-star) algorithm, can be employed to find the shortest or most efficient path for delivery vehicles. This approach minimizes delivery times and fuel costs, enhancing overall service efficiency

A* Algorithm for Route Optimization

Find the shortest or most efficient path from the warehouse to the delivery point.Use the A* algorithm, which combines the advantages of Dijkstra's algorithm and a heuristic approach, to efficiently compute the optimal delivery route
